A little bit about me

I'm a product designer interested in designs that focus on inclusion and diversity . I want to create meaningful experiences that resonate with their values. Inclusion to me means designing access for all and minimizing the gap between differences and limitations.

I'm currently a senior at UC Berkeley studying architecture . I love finding intersections of 2-D and 3-D designs and hope to make design more accessible. I am currently interning as a product designers and am actively looking to find a full time design role!
